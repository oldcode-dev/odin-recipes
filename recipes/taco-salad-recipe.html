Taco Salad with Lime Vinegar Dressing

This taco salad is a fresh and flavorful dish perfect for lunch or dinner. The lime vinegar dressing adds a zesty kick that complements the ingredients beautifully.

Serves: 4

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Salad

Author: Ernest

Date: 2023-10-01

Source: Example Recipe Site

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 head of lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Lime Vinegar Dressing

  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning to the beef and follow package instructions.
  3. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, black beans, and avocado.
  4. Add the cooked beef to the salad mixture.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  7. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.
