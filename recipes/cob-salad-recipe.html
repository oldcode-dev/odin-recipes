Cob Salad

Home

This cob salad is a hearty and colorful dish that combines fresh vegetables, proteins, and a delicious dressing. Perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

Serves: 4

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Salad

Author: Ernest

Date: 2023-10-01

Source: All Recipes

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup bacon bits
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, chicken, bacon bits, blue cheese, hard-boiled eggs, and avocado.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your cob salad!
